Important Rental Requirements

Please review the following requirements before submitting your rental application.

📅

6 Month Minimum Term

All rental agreements require a minimum commitment of 6 months. After the initial 6 months, your agreement will convert to a month-to-month arrangement.

💳

Payment Method

All payments must be made via debit or credit card. We accept all major cards including Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and Discover.

💰

First Payment

Your first payment is due on the day of installation. Our technician will confirm successful installation before processing your initial payment.

By submitting a rental application, you agree to these requirements and the terms of our rental agreement. Please contact us if you have any questions about our requirements or policies.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our rental service

What areas do you service? ▼
We currently service Duval County, St. John's County, Clay County, and Nassau County. If you're outside these areas, please contact us to see if we can accommodate your location.
What's included in the maintenance coverage? ▼
Our maintenance coverage includes all repairs and parts replacement at no additional cost. If your appliance stops working properly, we'll send a technician to fix or replace it free of charge.
How long are the rental agreements? ▼
All rentals require a 6-month minimum commitment. After the initial term, you can continue renting month-to-month with no long-term contract required.
Can I upgrade my appliances during my rental period? ▼
Yes! You can upgrade to a different model or add additional appliances at any time during your rental period. Contact us to discuss upgrade options.
What payment methods do you accept? ▼
We accept all major credit and debit cards including Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and Discover. Payments are processed securely on the day of installation and monthly thereafter.
